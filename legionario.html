Kit de habilidades (Warmonger — Legionnary)

Q · Shield Bash
The Legionary strikes enemies with their shield, dealing [pd] = (80 + 100%[ap] + 50% [armor]). The impact knocks back enemies and makes them vulnerable for 5 seconds. If the ability hits an enemy casting a spell, it interrupts the spell and puts it on cooldown. Causes 130% aggro. Weapon Specialization Bonus +12% aggro generation for this ability. [mana] cost is increased by 10% of your [ap].
W · Catapult
The Legionary throws the nearest ally or enemy to a target area. All enemies in that area take [pd] = (8%target Max Health (Max 800 versus monsters) + 5% [ap]). The tossed enemy also takes damage and stuns all vulnerable enemies for 2 seconds within a 2.5 radius. The tossed ally takes no damage. Makes 130% aggro Weapon Specialization Bonus +0.2 meters to throw distance. [mana] cost is increased by 10% of your [ap].
E · Spear Throw
The Legionary readies and throws a spear in a target direction. The throw gains extra range, damage, and mana cost the longer the Legionary spends preparing to launch the spear. It deals [pd] = (100 + 100% [ap]) to all enemies in its path. If the spear hits a vulnerable enemy, it taunts them, forcing them to attack the Legionary for 2 seconds. Taunts monsters even if they are not vulnerable. Weapon Specialization Bonus +0.3 meters to spear throw distance. [mana] cost is increased by 10% of your [ap].
T · Iron Skin
Each time the Legionary hits a target with an attack, they gain 1% [armor] and 1% [mr] for 10 seconds. The effect stacks up to 10 times. When the Legionary has 10 stacks part of the Legionary's [armor] and [mr] is converted into bonus [ap] (5% [armor] + 5% [mr]). Weapon Specialization Bonus +0.5% to total armor resistance.
R · Unstoppable
The Legionary removes all crowd control effects from themselves and applies a buff for 10 seconds. During the buff, the Legionary gains bonuses to movement speed of 10% + 5% [ap] and reduces incoming damage by 30%. For each target killed or a target in which the Warmonger participated in the kill, they restore 10% of their missing health. While Unstoppable is active, the Legionary generates 100% more aggro. [mana] cost is increased by 10% of your [ap].

Por qué: r2 requiere un aliado (redirige daño), inútil en solitario. r1 convierte armor/mr en AP durante el ultimate — puro daño.
